Lori Jardine,

Background

Helping teens & families
find their way forward

Lori is a Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner and Family Nurse Practitioner dedicated to helping teens and families move toward healing and growth. She specializes in working with adolescents struggling with ADHD, Autism, ODD, DMDD, PTSD, anxiety, and depression. Her approach is compassionate, collaborative, and focused on the whole person.

Lori has worked closely with Psychiatric Nurse Practitioners, Psychologists, and Primary Care providers across community health settings, family practice, adult corrections, youth corrections, and inpatient behavioral medicine — building the kind of cross-disciplinary experience that sets her apart.

She believes strength is built by doing hard things, and that her life's purpose is to inspire others to reach their greatest potential. If you're finished struggling with medication management, need a personalized treatment plan, or are seeking alternative treatment options — Lori is here to help.

Lori is bilingual in English and Spanish, and welcomes Spanish-speaking patients.

Education & Career

The journey to
Well-I-AM

Early Career

Registered Nurse — Cardiac & Neurology

Began her nursing career in cardiac and neurology care, developing a strong clinical foundation and a whole-person perspective on health that continues to shape her practice today.

7 Years

Correctional Nurse — Washington County Sheriff's Office

Spent seven years providing nursing care in a correctional setting, gaining deep experience with underserved populations, complex mental health presentations, and the critical importance of dignified, consistent care.

Across Settings

Multi-Specialty Collaboration

Collaborated with specialists across primary care, urgent care, wound care, pulmonology, and acute inpatient mental health — building the cross-disciplinary perspective that defines her approach to medication management today.

Advanced Training

PMHNP-BC — Psychiatric Specialization

Pursued advanced graduate training in psychiatric mental health, earning board certification as a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner. Completed clinical rotations across inpatient, outpatient, and telehealth psychiatric settings.

Founded

Well-I-AM Mental Health

Launched Well-I-AM to create the practice she always wished existed — where patients feel heard, care is demystified, and getting help feels like a relief, not an ordeal.
