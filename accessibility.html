Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: April 2026

Well-I-AM Mental Health is committed to ensuring that our website and services are accessible to all individuals, including those with disabilities. We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

Our Efforts

We have taken the following steps to make our website accessible:

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy for screen reader navigation.
  • Alternative text descriptions on all meaningful images.
  • ARIA labels on interactive elements such as navigation menus and buttons.
  • Keyboard-navigable menus and interactive components.
  • Responsive design that adapts to different screen sizes and devices.
  • Sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds.
  • Support for reduced-motion preferences for users sensitive to animations.

Third-Party Content

Our website integrates third-party services, including the SimplePractice scheduling widget and Google Fonts. While we strive to ensure all content is accessible, we cannot guarantee the accessibility of third-party content. SimplePractice maintains its own accessibility practices for the client portal and scheduling tools.

Bilingual Services

We are proud to offer services in both English and Spanish. Se habla español. If you need assistance in Spanish, please contact us by phone at 435-500-6529 or by email.

Alternative Ways to Access Our Services

If you encounter any barriers using our website, we are happy to assist you through alternative means:

  • Phone: Call us at 435-500-6529 to schedule an appointment, ask questions, or request information.
  • Email: Contact us at lori.jardine@well-i-am.net for any assistance.
  • In person: Visit our office at 393 E Riverside Drive, Suite 102, St. George, UT 84790 (available Fridays).

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of this website. If you encounter accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Phone: 435-500-6529
  • Email: lori.jardine@well-i-am.net

We take accessibility concerns seriously and will make reasonable efforts to address reported issues promptly.
