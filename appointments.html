Who Are You?

Info for new & existing patients

📋

What to Expect

Your first visit is a 50-minute comprehensive evaluation covering your psychiatric history, current symptoms, medications, lifestyle, and treatment goals.

📄

Intake Paperwork

After booking, you'll receive intake forms via the SimplePractice client portal. Complete them at least 24 hours before your appointment so Lori can review them in advance.

💳

Payment

Well-I-AM accepts Medicare and Aetna. Self-pay rates are also available — $120 for the initial appointment, $80 for monthly follow-ups. We accept all major credit cards and HSA/FSA cards.

🔄

Follow-Up Visits

Routine follow-up visits are 20–30 minutes. If you're experiencing significant changes or new symptoms, please let us know when booking so we can schedule additional time.

💊

Prescription Refills

Refills require an active appointment within the past 90 days. If you're running low, book a follow-up before you run out — don't wait until the last dose.

📱

Client Portal

Manage your appointments, messages, and billing through the SimplePractice client portal. Sign in to your portal here.
