Common Questions

Frequently asked

Do you accept new patients?
Yes! We welcome new patients and typically offer appointments within 1–2 weeks for new evaluations.
Do you offer telehealth?
Yes. Telehealth appointments are available across Utah and Nevada through secure, HIPAA-compliant video. In-person visits are also available on Fridays at our St. George office.
How long are appointments?
Initial evaluations are 50 minutes. Follow-up medication management visits are typically 20–30 minutes.
What's the difference between a PMHNP and a psychiatrist?
A Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner holds an advanced practice nursing degree with specialized psychiatric training and can prescribe medications, diagnose conditions, and manage treatment — covering the same essential functions as a psychiatrist for most patients. The key distinction: psychiatrists hold a medical doctorate (MD or DO) and are uniquely qualified to perform official diagnostic evaluations required for disability determinations, specialized programs, and certain legal or institutional assessments. If you need that level of documentation, we’ll let you know upfront and refer you to the right resource.
