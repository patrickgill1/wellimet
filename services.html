Anxiety & Depression

Anxiety and depression are among the most treatable mental health conditions — but they often go unaddressed or undertreated. Lori provides thorough assessment and personalized medication management to help you break the cycle and reclaim your daily life.

Treatment is tailored to your specific presentation, lifestyle, and history. From SSRIs and SNRIs to adjunctive therapies, every recommendation comes with a clear explanation and monitoring plan.

ADHD Management

ADHD looks different in every person. Lori conducts thorough evaluations to understand your unique presentation before recommending a treatment approach — stimulant, non-stimulant, or a combination — that fits your life, not just a checklist.

Adult ADHD is consistently underdiagnosed, and the right medication can be genuinely life-changing. Lori brings both clinical rigor and personal attention to every ADHD evaluation and follow-up.

Conditions Treated
ADHD-Inattentive ADHD-Hyperactive ADHD-Combined Executive Function Issues Adult ADHD Pediatric ADHD

Mood Disorders

Bipolar spectrum conditions and mood disorders require nuanced, expert management. Lori has extensive experience with mood stabilizers, atypical antipsychotics, and the complex medication balancing act that these conditions often demand.

She understands that mood disorders affect not just the individual, but relationships, work, and every aspect of daily life — and she builds treatment plans accordingly.

Conditions Treated
Bipolar I & II Cyclothymia Schizoaffective Disorder Mood Dysregulation Mixed Episodes

PTSD & Trauma

Trauma-informed psychiatric care begins with safety, trust, and patience. Lori uses evidence-based pharmacotherapy to reduce symptoms like hyperarousal, nightmares, and emotional dysregulation — coordinating closely with your therapist for a comprehensive approach.

She recognizes that trauma manifests differently for everyone, and her assessment process honors that complexity without asking you to relive what you've been through unnecessarily.

Conditions Treated
PTSD Complex Trauma (C-PTSD) Acute Stress Disorder Trauma-Related Insomnia Hyperarousal

Medication Management

Starting a medication is just the beginning. Lori provides consistent, attentive ongoing management — monitoring for effectiveness and side effects, adjusting dosages, managing transitions between medications, and coordinating with your pharmacy and other providers.

She also sees patients who are dissatisfied with their current psychiatric medications and want a fresh, thorough review and second opinion.

What's Included
Prescription Management Dosage Optimization Side Effect Monitoring Medication Reviews Second Opinions Provider Coordination

What to expect at
your visit

📋

Initial Evaluation — 50 min

A thorough first visit covering your psychiatric history, current symptoms, medications, lifestyle, and goals. This forms the foundation of your personalized treatment plan.

📄

Your Treatment Plan

After the evaluation, you'll receive a clear written plan — what we're treating, what medication is recommended, what to expect, and when to follow up. No surprises.

🔄

Follow-Up Visits — 20–30 min

Regular check-ins to assess medication effectiveness, address side effects, adjust dosages, and update your plan as needed. Frequency depends on your situation.
